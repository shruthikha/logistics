Features of Imp-Vise

Route Optimization

The system identifies the most efficient routes, minimizing travel time, fuel consumption, and delays while ensuring timely deliveries.

Eco-Friendly Performance

By estimating emissions for each route, we actively contribute to reducing the carbon footprint of your logistics operations, promoting sustainability.

Safety & Compliance

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-Being - Max safe speed monitoring based on truck load ensures real-time alerts to prevent accidents, prioritizing driver safety.

SDG 5: Gender Equality - The system features an SOS button designed to prioritize the safety of female truck drivers, ensuring quick responses during emergencies.

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure - QR-based document retrieval (RC book, license, insurance) streamlines the inspection process and reduces delays during inspections, leading to improved compliance and efficiency.

Goal

To enhance travel efficiency, improve customer satisfaction, and contribute to environmental sustainability.

Environmental Sustainability

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities & Communities

SDG 13: Climate Action

Real-time AQI Tracking: The system tracks Air Quality Index (AQI) in real-time, helping drivers determine if travel is safe in a given area and avoid locations with poor air quality.

CO₂ Emission Estimation: Our system estimates CO₂ emissions for each route, promoting eco-friendly logistics and contributing to a greener environment.

Live Hospital Location Tracking: In case of emergencies, the system provides live tracking of nearby hospital locations, ensuring immediate access to medical care when needed.

Time & Maintenance Efficiency

SDG 8: Decent Work & Economic Growth

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Automated Compliance Alerts: The system sends automated alerts for insurance and fitness certificate (FC) renewals, preventing compliance issues and reducing the risk of fines or penalties.

Fleet Efficiency Optimization: Live tracking and real-time alerts optimize fleet efficiency, minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity, ultimately reducing operational costs.

These features collectively aim to enhance operational efficiency, promote environmental sustainability, and ensure the safety and well-being of both drivers and communities.
